About Hack Against Violence

Brussels, Paris, Orlando, Beirut, Baghdad, Ankara, Istanbul, Nice, Dallas and many more… prove that everyone is affected by violence.

Context

Next to killing thousands of innocent people, acts of violence have given rise to even more unrest and exclusion across societies.

There is a time to reflect and mourn for the victims of these attacks and there is a time to act!
Do you want to move beyond sharing “#prayForInsertTragicEventHere” and apply your skills to come up with practical ideas on how to prevent violence and encourage interaction among social groups?

Practical

Dev Bootcamp and invite you to join us for a weekend of building innovative solutions that aim to make an impact towards reducing violence.

We'll kick off Friday, September 16th at 6:30 pm, and wrap up mid day on the 18th, at the Devbootcamp campus in San Francisco. Please check out this for full details as well as RSVP (you must RSVP through this page to confirm your seat).
